# Project Summary
This project is a web application designed to assist researchers in managing their academic literature and facilitating the exploration of relevant papers. It provides features for defining reading purposes, logging search records, and expanding knowledge through references and citations. The application aims to enhance the research workflow by integrating various functionalities, such as tracking research questions, managing notes, and visualizing relationships between topics.

# Project Module Description
- **Purpose Workspace**: Allows users to define their reading purposes, including custom inputs for premium users.
- **Entry Paper Workspace**: Facilitates the addition of search records, keywords, and candidate papers with options to mark relevance and track discovery paths.
- **Read Workspace**: Provides functionality for reading and annotating papers, including literature and permanent notes management.
- **Expand Workspace**: Enables users to manage references, cited papers, and similar topics/methods, with options for manual and auto-identification.
- **Visualize Workspace**: Offers visualization of research insights and candidate research questions.
- **Draft Workspace**: Supports drafting research papers with structured checks for content and citations.
